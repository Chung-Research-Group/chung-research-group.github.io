Research. 연구주제

We develop theories and computational systems that connect electronic and atomistic information, materials data, mixture adsorption, cyclic process performance, and autonomous scientific reasoning within an end-to-end framework.

전자·원자 수준의 정보와 소재 데이터를 다성분 흡착, 순환식 분리공정 성능과 자율적 과학 추론으로 연결하는 end-to-end 이론과 계산 시스템을 개발합니다.

01

Digital materials intelligence

디지털 소재 인텔리전스

We build and deploy high-throughput digital workflows (CoRE MOF Tools, AIM) to curate, screen and evaluate thousands of nanoporous materials, creating digital twins for accurate and rapid performance prediction.

02

Data & AI for materials and chemicals

소재·화학을 위한 데이터와 AI

We build foundational platforms (CoRE MOF DB, PACMAN, MOFClassifier, GWP-estimator) to enable autonomous discovery of next-generation materials and chemical systems.

03

Diffusion, adsorption & reaction at the nanoscale

나노스케일의 확산·흡착·반응

We investigate the fundamentals of adsorption (e.g., BET analysis), diffusion, and reaction mechanisms to design and understand materials and chemicals for carbon capture, conversion, and energy storage.
