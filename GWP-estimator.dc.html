Software & Data / Machine learning

GWP-estimator.

A machine learning model to predict the atmospheric lifetime and the global warming potential (GWP) of molecules. The developed models were used to screen more than 7,000 molecules in PubChem and bigQM7 data sets in a search for SF₆ replacement gases for the electric industry.
