How to apply — 지원 방법

Email Prof. Chung with (1) your CV, (2) a short paragraph on why you are interested in our group, and (3) your transcript. Mention specific projects or papers of ours that caught your attention.

지원 서류: CV, 관심 연구 분야를 담은 짧은 자기소개, 성적표. 관심 있는 논문이나 프로젝트를 함께 언급해 주세요.

We host visiting students and researchers and welcome new collaborations in computational materials discovery, adsorption, and machine learning. Reach out with a brief note describing your interests and timeline.
